Repetition makes masters. Part of the problem with imposter syndrome, especially in a field like tech, is how easy it is to feel like you're not going anywhere. IterRATE is here to solve that. Non-invasive tracking of your coding journey.

How much experience do you have? Iterate tracks the exact time you spend on Leetcode.com, signed in. If you move to another tab, it'll stop tracking. At the end of each session, it'll add your total up and let you know your progress. We've set a goal for 100 hours, which is a good benchmark for beginers.
